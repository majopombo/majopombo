About me

Design meets data

Through internships at Caterpillar Inc., I’ve worked across analytics and UX, building data tools, conducting user research, and designing experiences for real users in industrial spaces. I’m especially drawn to the intersection of business, technology, and human experience. Outside of work, I care deeply about leadership, creativity, community, and faith, and I want the things I build to serve people well and make a meaningful impact.
